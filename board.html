The How

MCP is how we connect everything.

The Model Context Protocol turns our disconnected systems into one fabric — that agents can read, reason over, and act across.

  • Automate marketing campaigns end-to-end — from brief to live to optimised, across platforms.
  • Finish automating the small stuff — the spreadsheets and Excel sheets running around the business get absorbed.
  • Stop the platform-hopping — the team stops shuttling data between systems by hand.
  • The systems that don't talk to each other — finally talk.
MCP is the practical answer to VCG's oldest problem: "our systems don't integrate." It's the connective tissue that lets one agent operate across CRM, MT5, PSPs, BI, and the back-office. This is where a lot of the day-to-day toil quietly disappears.
The Bigger Idea

The real prize isn't the model.
It's the loop.

Models are becoming commodities. The advantage is the proprietary learning loop you build on top of them — and own.

  • Human capital — our people's judgment, relationships and pattern recognition. Token capital — the AI capability we build and own. They compound together.
  • You can offload a task — even a job — but you can never offload your learning. The firm that compounds learning across people and AI wins.
  • Architect it so we can swap the model and keep the veteran — our IP lives in our knowledge and data, not in someone else's weights.

Framing inspired by Satya Nadella's “human capital × token capital” thesis, June 2026.

The strategic reframe. The board takeaway: we are not betting on a model — we are building the compounding loop that becomes VCG's owned IP. This sets up the Knowledge Vault as the place that loop lives. Human capital becomes MORE valuable as token capital grows; people set the direction, AI compounds it.

How We Do It Responsibly

  • Human-in-the-loop on every write: AI proposes; people approve.
  • AI narrates — it never touches the numbers.
